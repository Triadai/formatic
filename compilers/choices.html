compiler.choices

Normalizes the choices for a field. Supports the following formats.

'red, blue'

['red', 'blue']

{red: 'Red', blue: 'Blue'}

[{value: 'red', label: 'Red'}, {value: 'blue', label: 'Blue'}]

All of those formats are normalized to:

[{value: 'red', label: 'Red'}, {value: 'blue', label: 'Blue'}]
